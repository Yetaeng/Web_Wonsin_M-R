Company Introduction

Home > Introduction > Introduction
Wonsin M&R Co., Ltd. was founded on June 1, 1984. We have localized bearing heaters for the first time in Korea, and are well received in Korea, and we are striving to improve quality today.

Wonsin M&R Co., Ltd. was founded on June 1, 1984, and imported bearing heaters from Germany and Japan for about two years. In 1986, bearing heaters were first localized and sold in Korea.

It has also been exported to China and Taiwan, and is also being supplied to sites in Korea and other countries, such as Southeast Asia, the Middle East, China, and South America, as well as plant export sites.
